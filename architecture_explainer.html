The Beginner View

The 5-Step Pipeline

Under the hood, the agent follows a strict logical pipeline to guarantee high-quality results. It operates like a tiny team of researchers.

0

Intent Analysis

The AI understands exactly how many articles you want and what the core subject is.

1

Query Planning

Instead of just Googling exactly what you typed, the AI brainstorms 5 different search angles to cast a wide net.

2

Search & Collect

It queries DuckDuckGo and Yahoo News, collecting URLs while skipping known junk domains (like MSN or social media).

3

Web Scraping

A virtual browser visits every website, bypasses bot-blockers, and extracts only the actual article text (ignoring ads/menus).

4

Semantic Ranking

It mathematically compares every article to your original prompt, keeping only the most relevant ones and throwing away duplicates.

5

Final Summary

The AI writes a cohesive synthesis of the selected articles into the final answer.

Adapting the LLM-Scraper Architecture for Pipelines & Production

1. Automated Asset Scavenger

ENVIRONMENT & LOOKDEV

The Bottleneck

Environment artists spend hours digging through Poly Haven, TurboSquid, Sketchfab, and forums looking for specific PBR textures, HDRIs, or base meshes with suitable commercial licenses.

01
Intent Planner
Expands visual prompt into technical search queries (e.g. "worn red brick seamless 4k site:polyhaven.com")
02
Search APIs
Hits targeted indexes (ArtStation, Sketchfab, private studio libraries if indexed).
03
Playwright Scrape
Extracts asset descriptions, topology stats (polycount), and crucial License Types (CC0, CC-BY).
04
Semantic Filter
Compares scraped mesh/material tags against the artist's original prompt to filter out irrelevant hits.
05
Curated Output
Synthesizes a structured list of vetted direct-download links organized by licensing.
Final Output (JSON/Markdown) [CC0 - FREE COMMERCIAL]
- Worn Victorian Brick (8K PBR): https://polyhaven.com/...
- Industrial Factory Wall Material: https://ambientcg.com/...

[PAID / ROYALTIES APPLY]
- 3D Scanned Crumbling Brick (TurboSquid - $15): ...

2. Tech Art Troubleshooting Oracle

PIPELINE & TD SUPPORT

The Bottleneck

Technical Directors face obscure stack traces in Maya/Houdini/UE5. The solutions are buried in 10-year-old Reddit threads, GitHub issues, or Unreal Engine forum arguments.

01
Log Parsing
Accepts raw crash logs or cryptic errors ("V-Ray UNHANDLED EXCEPTION in render thread").
02
Targeted Search
Queries restrict to specific domains (forums.unrealengine.com, github.com, stackoverflow.com).
03
Deep Scrape
Playwright loads full multi-page forum threads and expands hidden comments to capture the entire discussion context.
04
Context Filter
Ranks threads specifically matching the user's DCC version and Operating System.
05
Solution Extractor
LLM identifies the actual working solution ("Edit: fixed it by...") ignoring the noise.
Final Output (Actionable Steps) ROOT CAUSE: V-Ray is running out of GPU VRAM while calculating displacement.

WORKAROUND STEPS:
1. Go to V-Ray Settings > Geometry > Displacement
2. Disable "Use Object Material"
3. Lower edge length from 4 to 2.

Source: https://forums.chaos.com/thread/... (User 'render_guy94' confirmed fix in Maya 2024)

3. Competitor Intelligence Tracker

BI & BIZ DEV

The Bottleneck

Game outsourcing studios and ArchViz firms need to know what tech stacks rival studios are adopting, what talent they are hiring, and what contracts they just won.

01
Topic Planner
Generates daily search queries based on rival studio names + "hiring" or "portfolio update".
02
Broad Search
Scans LinkedIn, ArtStation jobs, 80 Level, and industry press releases.
03
News Scrape
Extracts job requirement texts and project post-mortems/breakdowns.
04
Keyword Filter
Filters heavily for tech stack keywords (UE5, Gaussian Splatting, Houdini, USD pipeline).
05
Weekly Report
Synthesizes findings into a high-level briefing for the Studio Head or Lead TD.
Final Output (Intelligence Briefing) STUDIO: Blur Studio
ACTIVITY: Major shift to Unreal Engine 5 for linear animation detected.
EVIDENCE:
- Posted 3 job listings today requiring "UE5 Sequencer" and "Lumen lighting" experience.
- Published 80Level article breaking down real-time rendering on latest project.

ACTIONABLE: Competitor is matching our real-time rendering capabilities.
